Our Story

Born from a love
for good food

Emmai Restaurant opened its doors in 2023 with a simple belief — that food should feel like home. Nestled on the 2nd floor of the Dr Neilhouzhü Kire Complex at Razhü Point, Kohima, we built a space where the warmth of the interiors matches the heart in every dish we serve.

From our Chef's Favourites and hearty mains to freshly brewed hot beverages, every item on our menu is made with care. We blend the local spirit of Nagaland with global flavours — a menu as diverse as it is delicious.

Come for the food. Stay for the ambience. Leave with a memory.
